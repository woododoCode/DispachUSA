Answer: When an individual or business wants to post, they must register and make an account for posting only. Also, if an individual or business wants to search for goods/cargo to ship, they must register and make an account for searching only. If you're a broker then you can register as a broker and used both options of searching and posting.
Answer: Signing up is free, but to fully access the website and all of its information and be able to post and search for shipments you will have to choose one out of the 3 payment plan options. Answer: A broker is a person or firm who arranges transactions between someone who posts and the carrier for a commission when the deal is executed. A broker who also acts as a someone who posts or someone who searches for shipments becomes a principal party to the deal. Overall a broker is a middleman.
